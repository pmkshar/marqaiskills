# Output Format

Structure the delivered sequence as follows.

```markdown
# Cold Email Sequence: [Campaign Name]

**Campaign Details**:
- **Target Audience**: [ICP description]
- **Sequence Type**: [7-email classic / fast-track / etc.]
- **Duration**: [Total days]
- **Sender**: [From name and role]
- **Expected Reply Rate**: [X-X%]
```

## Sending Best Practices

- Tuesdays-Thursdays produce the highest open rates.
- 10-11 AM and 2-3 PM are optimal send times.
- Avoid Mondays (inbox overload) and Fridays (weekend mode).
- Send based on the recipient's local time zone.
