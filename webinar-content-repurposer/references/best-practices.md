# Best Practices and Usage

## Best Practices
1. Timestamp everything so video editors can locate clips.
2. Maintain context: each piece must stand alone without requiring the full webinar.
3. Attribute quotes: always credit speakers for authority.
4. Progressive disclosure: start with teasers, lead to deeper content.
5. Cross-promote: link between assets (blog, email, social).
6. SEO optimize: target keywords from the webinar topic in blog posts.
7. Brand consistency: maintain voice and branding across all assets.
8. Update links: ensure all webinar replay links work and track properly.

## Trigger Phrases
- "Repurpose this webinar into content"
- "Turn this transcript into blog posts and social media"
- "Create a content plan from this webinar recording"
- "Extract quotes and insights from this webinar"
- "Build an email sequence from this webinar"

## Example Request
> "Here's the transcript from our 45-minute webinar on 'Modern Data Security Practices.' Create a comprehensive content repurposing plan with blog posts, social media content, and email sequence."

## Response Strategy
1. Analyze transcript for key themes.
2. Identify best quotes and moments.
3. Create content asset breakdown.
4. Build distribution calendar.
5. Suggest quick wins for immediate publishing.

One hour of webinar content can fuel 3-4 weeks of multi-channel content when properly repurposed.
