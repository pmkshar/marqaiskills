# Output Template

Produce the content calendar in this structure. Replace every bracketed field with concrete, audience-specific content. Do not leave placeholders.

```markdown
# Social Selling Content Calendar

**Target Audience**: [Job titles/roles]
**Your Solution**: [What you sell]
**Content Goal**: [Attract/Educate/Engage]
**Posting Frequency**: [X] posts per week
**Duration**: 30 days

---

## Content Strategy

**Your Personal Brand Position**:
"[One-sentence positioning - e.g., 'I help B2B SaaS companies scale revenue without scaling headcount']"

**Content Themes**:
1. [Theme 1]: [Description]
2. [Theme 2]: [Description]
3. [Theme 3]: [Description]
4. [Theme 4]: [Description]

**Success Metrics**:
- Impressions: [Target]
- Engagement Rate: [Target %]
- Profile views: [Target]
- Connection requests: [Target]
- Inbound messages: [Target]

---

## 30-Day Content Calendar

### Week [N]: [Weekly Theme]

**Day [N] - [Weekday] ([Post Type])**

**Hook**: [One-line scroll-stopping opener]

**Post**:
```
[Full post body, formatted with line breaks, written in the user's voice]
```

**Why This Works**:
- [Reason 1]
- [Reason 2]
- [Reason 3]

**Best Time to Post**: [Day/time window]

---

[Repeat the Day block for all 30 posts, cycling through the content pillars and post types]
```

## Coverage Requirements
- Generate the full 30 posts, not a sample. Distribute across all content pillars and post types.
- Structure the 4 weeks as a progression: Week 1 Establish Authority, Week 2 Problem/Solution Focus, Week 3 Stories and Social Proof, Week 4 Scale and Conversion.
- See examples.md for worked post examples in each style.
