# Champion Development Plan

## Phase 1: Initial Contact (Week 1)

Goal: get the first meeting and establish rapport.
- Secure a warm intro from the mutual connection
- Send personalized outreach
- Schedule a discovery call
- Research their LinkedIn thoroughly

Success metric: meeting scheduled.

## Phase 2: Discovery and Qualification (Week 1-2)

Goal: confirm they feel the pain and can champion.
- Run the discovery call (see references/meeting-prep.md)
- Validate they have the pain
- Assess their influence and willingness
- Get them to open up about process and players

Success metric: they articulate the pain clearly and agree to a next step.

## Phase 3: Value Demonstration (Week 2-3)

Goal: show how the solution addresses their specific problem.
- Deliver a tailored demo focused on their pain points
- Share a relevant case study
- Calculate ROI specific to their situation
- Capture their reaction and feedback

Success metric: they say "this would really help us."

## Phase 4: Champion Activation (Week 3-4)

Goal: get them to actively advocate internally.
- Ask: "Based on what you've seen, would this help you?"
- Ask: "Who else should see this?"
- Ask: "Would you be comfortable introducing me to [decision maker]?"
- Provide ammunition to sell internally (ROI calc, one-pager)

Success metric: they introduce the economic buyer or invite others to the next meeting.

## Phase 5: Deal Progression (Ongoing)

Goal: keep them engaged throughout the deal.
- Hold weekly check-ins
- Ask for coaching on the internal process
- Get their input on the proposal
- Have them review and socialize internally
- Have them coach you on objections and competitors

Success metric: the deal moves forward with their help.
